Welcome to What2Cook. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

When you use What to Cook, we collect the following information:

  • Personal Information (with consent):
    - Your Google email address and name, when you sign in using Gmail.
  • App Usage Data:
    - Details of food items you create
    - Dates or times when food items are marked as consumed

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To identify your account and provide personalized service
  • To store and display food items you create within the app
  • To record and manage the consumption history of food items
  • To improve our application experience through feature optimization and usage analytics
  • To use anonymized and aggregated data for:
    • Statistical analysis
    • Product improvement
    • Marketing purposes, including content recommendations and promotional insights

Note: We will never sell your personal data or share it with third parties without your explicit consent.

3. Consent and Control

  • You must explicitly accept this Privacy Policy before using the app.
  • You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at mycoookbuddy@gmail.com.
  • You can stop using the app at any time, and your data will remain protected as per this policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Anonymized Data

We may analyze app usage patterns and food preferences across users. All such insights are generated using fully anonymized data, meaning your personal identity (name/email) is not linked to any analysis or marketing activity.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes and provide an option to review and accept any updated terms.
